Procedure

Step 1

Weigh out the ginger. Make sure to have the right amount for each group of ginger. You need cups with 20g, two cups with 40g, and two cups with 80g.


Step 2

Create the growing containers. Do this by inserting the smaller deli container with the larger one, and threading a cord through the small one. Also fill the larger one with water and the smaller one with soil and seeds.


Step 3

Place the growing containers under a lighting element so that they can grow. They should be in the layout that is shown in the image.


Step 4

As the seeds grow, record the heights of every plant. Do this every Friday until 35 days have passed. Check on the plants every day to monitor their health.


Step 5

Analyze your data. This can be done with ANOVA and t-tests.
